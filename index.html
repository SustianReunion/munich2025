Story of our journey

First reunion

First reunion

The maiden chapter of our SUSTian DE reunion unfolded in 2014, marking the beginning of a wonderful journey together. It all started in Karlsruhe, where a small but enthusiastic group of SUSTians gathered to reconnect, share memories, and celebrate our shared heritage. Over the years, our community has grown, and each reunion has become a cherished tradition, bringing together friends and families from across Europe. These gatherings are filled with laughter, adda, games, cultural performances, and, of course, delicious deshi food, strengthening the bonds within our SUSTian family.

Second reunion

Second reunion

We gathered for our second reunion in F√ºrth in 2018, marking another milestone in our growing SUSTian community. Friends and families traveled from different parts of Germany to join the celebration, making it a truly special occasion. The day was filled with cheerful adda, laughter, and a variety of cultural performances that highlighted our shared heritage. Everyone enjoyed a delicious spread of Bengali food, which added a comforting touch of home to the festivities.

Third reunion

Third reunion

Stuttgart welcomed us for our 3rd reunion in 2023, and it was truly a memorable event. Around 100 SUSTians and their family members attended, making it one of our largest gatherings yet. The atmosphere was vibrant, filled with laughter and the warmth of old friendships rekindled. We enjoyed lots of adda, fun games for both kids and adults, and a variety of cultural programs that showcased the diverse talents within our community. The highlight for many was the delicious Bangladeshi food, which brought a taste of home to everyone present.

Fourth reunion

Fourth reunion

This year, we are excited to organize our fourth reunion in Munich. The vibrant city will provide the perfect backdrop for reconnecting with old friends and welcoming new faces into our SUSTian family. We have planned a variety of activities, including cultural performances, games, and group outings to Eibsee, to ensure everyone has a memorable experience.
